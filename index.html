About Me

My research examines how credentialing reform, governance restructuring, and public investment in early childhood education reshape professional legitimacy and labor stratification.

Grounded in Critical Feminist Theory, Intersectionality, and Community Cultural Wealth, I employ qualitative methods including critical phenomenology and cross-role policy analysis.
